About us

The Executive Hair Additions staff have gained a reputation for being industry insiders and innovators. They've been training and educating
themselves in some of today's top hairstyling looks before social media influencers even picked them up! Trend-forecasting is kinda our thing, and we love working with beauty-savvy clients who want to recreate fashion week fads and red carpet styles! Not to mention, they're more than happy to pass on a styling tip, and hair product recommendation or two! So, don't be shy to pick their brain during your complementary image consultation.

If you didn't notice already, we're obsessed with the power of transformation. In fact, we're masters of the art of makeover! Our Executive Hair
Additions stylists love working with each client's specific tastes and favorite trends. We focus on finding your inner beauty and pulling together a hairstyle that's a reflection of it! Whether your ideal look is mermaid waves, subtle highlights, our waist-length extensions, our stylist passionately work to execute a look that's an extension of your personal style! Our vast skillset allows us to give each client beautiful hair with its own set of individualized intricacies and dimension. We create more than just a hairstyle, but a work of art!

We want to make sure the you leave the salon absolutely glowing! Even if your just dropping in for a brow wax or regular trim, our Executive
Hair Additions team makes sure that you enjoy everything that our luxury salon atmosphere has to offer. There's no such thing as service that's too small, and we're dedicated to solving all your beauty need in our glam sessions! After all, Executive Hair Additions was built to give our clients the ultimate treat yourself experience. Our mission is to making a lasting change in our clients' lives by showing them what a difference it can make when you take time to invest in your beauty and celebrate yourself.
