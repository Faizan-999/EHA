About Us

About
Executive Hair Additions

At Executive Hair Additions, our craft is our passion! Our master stylists have committed their careers to learning and perfecting the industry’s latest techniques to better serve the clients in their chair. Your every hair wish is our command, and we’re confident that we have the skill set to execute any look and inspiration image that you might have. (That’s how we acquired our name actually).

Executive Hair Additions was founded by a team of entrepreneurial hairstylists who were hungry to learn every facet of the beauty industry. It’s their passion and their dedication to making clients beautiful that brings life to the salon!

From the hottest new looks in balayage hair color to voluminous hair extensions, our stylists specialize in some of today’s top hairstyling trends! We’re home to some of the most buzzed about stylists in all of South Florida, and our family of clients come from all over the region to experience the best of the best in specialty services. We take pride in our work, and each hairstyle we create defines the term no hair out of place.
