Hair Restoration

SHORT HISTORY

We know that hair loss for women can be a sensitive subject, and sometimes even
taboo. Whether your condition is genetic, menopausal, or from chemotherapy and
other medical treatments, our team emphatically listen to each clients' story, ind
ividualized needs, and hair restoration goals. Making you feel comfortable is our
first priority! All of our hair restoration treatments are administered in a private
room with a seasoned service provider.


Unlike other commercial units, Executive Hair Additions custom blends Hair Sys
tems to fit each client's individual hair texture, color, length, and desired density.
Each unit is masterfully applied to blend seamlessly with your natural hair for un
detectable results. After your hair integration treatment, Executive Hair can
be cut, colored, and styled to completely restore the look that you once had!

We Offer All Hair System Types

Skin Base Systems | Lace Front Base Systems |Mono-filament Base Systems

And All Hair Integration Methods

Acrylic Adhesives | Silicone Adhesives | Water Based Adhesives | Tape-Ins.
